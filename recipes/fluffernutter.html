Fluffernutter

Description

The best sandwich ever. Pure joy and nostalgia.

Ingreidients

  • Two slices of bread
  • Spreadable Marshmallow Fluff
  • Peanut Butter

Steps

  1. Put slices of bread on plate, open faced
  2. Spread PB on one slice
  3. Spread Fluff on the other
  4. Put slices together
